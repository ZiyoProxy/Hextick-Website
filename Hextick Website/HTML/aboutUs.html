About US

What Are We?

Hextick is a watch company that combines style and quality. Hextick watches are built to complement every moment. We always prioritize quality over quantity which led us to limited products available which provides a top-notch item with great Appearance.

Our Company

Hextick is a concept of a watch company developed as part of a college project.The brand name "Hextick" represents precision ("hex") and clock hands ("tick"). Our team is a group of dedicated college students passionate about blending creativity and innovation. We curate the most limited edition watches with discounts. Each member brings unique skills, design, and research, making Hextick a unique & strong brand.
